The Harris Refurbishment

After the success of the Comeng's introduction, The Met chose it would refurbish their Harris Series 2 to look like the Comeng with these changes:

  • Cab redesign to look like a Comeng
  • Fabric half-back seats
  • Carpetted floors
  • Motorised doors


The Train that became a ghost

when the refurbished trains made it to the network, it was found with the weakend Series 2 motors, it struggled climbing hills, so the refurbishment was cancelled, and the ones which were refurbished were limited to the Sandringham, Port Melbourne and St Kilda lines.
