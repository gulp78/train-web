general info

The Harris Series 1 was the first of the Harris's to be put into service, boasting a new door layout and floorplan, these revolutionised the Melbourne network

Differences from the Taits

The Harris introduced quite a few new features which included:

  • 3 doorways, which were much wider
  • A new modern floor plan
  • Curved body (no more Clerestory)
  • Bigger windows
