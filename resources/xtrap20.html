2019 concept

With the Comeng fleet starting to show their age, Transport for Victoria got Alstom to make concept designs for the Comeng replacements with these new trains being known as the X'trapolis 2.0, Alstom were quick to come out with designs, but TfV didn't create new contract for Alstom to continue operation risking a lot of lost jobs.


2021 plan

With the new Victorian budget 2021-2022 it was revealed that the State Government were putting aside almost $1 billion for a budget boost to the public transport network, one of the projects was 25 new X'trapolis 2.0 with a slightly modified version of the original concept for the trains which are set to start running by the ETA 2024.
